We Know The Data

We are dedicated to know more about your clients and make sure that information will help you make their experience more pleasant. With that in mind, we created special software called TappyGo. The software keeps record of the ways transactions were made. You as a user may add notes to every transaction with any suggestions from you or your customers. All the data goes straight to our research team, which keeps track of everything.

The first step is to precess all the data and to decide what next steps to take. To do this, we conduct an internal and external assessment to understand what is happening with the payment methods and how they develop. A lot depends on the data collection, but we also keep in mind the nature and size of the organization we are working with to ensure that we keep it more personal. That helps us to make sure we are up to date.
